Never Give Me the Odds

By Joey Trull
Sources: Star Wars, NBCNews, Review Journal and Funny2 Odds

According to R2-D2 and translated by C-3PO, the odds of surviving a night on the planet Hoth are 1 out of 725. (Star Wars: Episode V – The Empire Strikes Back)

You have a better chance of being born with 11 fingers and toes, known as polydactylism, with odds of 1 out of 500, than surviving a night on Hoth. The chances of being killed while crossing the street are higher than surviving on Hoth as well at 1 out of 701.

1 : 725

According to C-3PO, the odds of successfully navigating the Hoth asteroid belt are 1 out of 3,720. (Star Wars: Episode V – The Empire Strikes Back)

You have a better chance of being injured while mowing the lawn, with odds of 1 out of 3,623, than navigating the asteroid belt. The chances of falling and dying in the shower are higher than navigating the asteroid belt as well at 1 out of 2,232.

1 : 3,720

According to Han Solo, the odds of the Death Star killshot are 1 out of 1,000,000. (Star Wars: Episode IV – A New Hope)

You have a better chance of winning an Olympic gold medal in your lifetime, with odds of 1 out of 662,000. The chances of being killed by lightning and meteorite strikes are higher than destroying the Death Star as well at 1 out of 700,000.

1 : 1,000,000
